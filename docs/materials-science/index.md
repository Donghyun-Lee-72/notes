# Materials Science

Materials science connects **processing, structure, properties, and
performance**. This section develops that chain from atomic bonding and crystal
structure through microstructure, thermodynamics, kinetics, characterization,
and engineering behavior.

## Characterization

- [Electron Microscopy: SEM and TEM](../learn/electron-microscopy-sem-tem.md) —
  electron–matter interactions, scattering and signal generation, imaging,
  diffraction, spectroscopy, specimen preparation, artifacts, and method
  selection.

## Planned map

- Atomic structure, bonding, defects, and diffusion
- Phase equilibria, phase diagrams, and materials thermodynamics
- Mechanical, electronic, optical, magnetic, and thermal properties
- X-ray diffraction, reciprocal space, structure factors, and pair correlations
- Surfaces, interfaces, thin films, and nanostructures
