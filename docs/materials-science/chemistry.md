# Chemistry

Chemistry explains matter through composition, atomic and molecular structure,
bonding, energy, reaction, and transformation. Within this shared section,
chemistry provides the molecular and mechanistic basis for understanding how
materials are synthesized, characterized, changed, and used.

## Planned map

- Atomic and molecular structure and chemical bonding
- Thermodynamics, equilibrium, kinetics, and reaction mechanisms
- Inorganic, organic, physical, and materials chemistry
- Synthesis, separation, spectroscopy, and analytical measurement
- Electrochemistry, surfaces, interfaces, and catalysis

These topics connect directly to the processing–structure–properties–performance
framework used in materials science.
