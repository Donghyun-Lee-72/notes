# 참고문헌

AI Notes는 공개된 원 논문, 벤치마크 논문, 표준, 공식 프로젝트 문서를 우선합니다. 개별 문서에서는 각 출처가 어떤 주장이나 설계 판단을 뒷받침하는지 설명합니다.

## 언어 모델과 Transformer

- Vaswani et al. (2017), [Attention Is All You Need](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762)
- Sennrich et al. (2016), [Neural Machine Translation of Rare Words with Subword Units](https://arxiv.org/abs/1508.07909)
- Kudo and Richardson (2018), [SentencePiece](https://arxiv.org/abs/1808.06226)
- Brown et al. (2020), [Language Models are Few-Shot Learners](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.14165)
- Liu et al. (2023), [Lost in the Middle](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.03172)

## 검색과 임베딩

- Reimers and Gurevych (2019), [Sentence-BERT](https://arxiv.org/abs/1908.10084)
- Karpukhin et al. (2020), [Dense Passage Retrieval](https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.04906)
- Lewis et al. (2020), [Retrieval-Augmented Generation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.11401)
- Thakur et al. (2021), [BEIR](https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.08663)
